## Detailed Marking Guide

### Part 1: Electrical Components (20 marks)

#### Question 1: Component Identification (16 marks)
- **4 marks per component:** Complete and accurate description of function and importance
- **3 marks per component:** Good description but missing some detail
- **2 marks per component:** Basic description
- **1 mark per component:** Attempted but incorrect
- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 2: Power Supply (4 marks)
- **1 mark:** Correct voltage
- **1 mark:** Correct current
- **2 marks:** Clear explanation of importance

**Mark:** /20

---

### Part 2: Wiring Basics (20 marks)

#### Question 3: Power Connections (8 marks)
- **3 marks:** All three connections correct
- **2 marks:** Two connections correct
- **1 mark:** One connection correct
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Correct answers:**
1. Positive/VCC
2. Negative/VSS
3. Ground/GND

- **5 marks:** Clear explanation of difference between ground and negative

#### Question 4: Data Connections (12 marks)
- **3 marks:** Correct communication type (serial)
- **4 marks:** Clear explanation of daisy-chain
- **5 marks:** Accurate diagram showing daisy-chain connections

**Mark:** /20

---

### Part 3: Control Concepts (20 marks)

- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 6: Control in Robot Arm (8 marks)
- **4 marks per answer:** Clear and correct explanation
- **3 marks per answer:** Mostly correct explanation
- **2 marks per answer:** Basic understanding shown
- **0 marks:** No answer

### Part 4: Servo Feedback (15 marks)

#### Question 7: Feedback Types (7 marks)

## Overall Performance Levels

### Excellent (85-100%)
- Comprehensive understanding of all electrical concepts
- Accurate identification and measurement of all components
- Clear analysis of control systems and feedback
- Complete, accurate wiring diagram
- All practical exercises completed to high standard

### Good (70-84%)
- Good understanding with minor gaps
- Mostly accurate identification and measurement
- Good analysis with minor gaps
- Good wiring diagram with minor omissions
- Most practical exercises completed well

### Satisfactory (55-69%)
- Basic understanding evident but some confusion
- Basic identification with some errors
- Basic analysis completed
- Basic wiring diagram completed
- Practical exercises completed but may lack detail

### Needs Improvement (Below 55%)
- Limited understanding, significant gaps
- Struggles with identification and measurement
- Limited or incorrect analysis
- Incomplete or incorrect wiring diagram
- Practical exercises incomplete or not attempted
