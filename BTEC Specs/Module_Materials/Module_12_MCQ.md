# Module 12: Automation Cell Design
## Multiple Choice Questions

**Instructions:** Choose the best answer for each question.

---

**1. What is an automation cell?**
   a) Just the robot
   b) A complete workstation with robot, fixtures, and safety systems
   c) The controller only
   d) The end effector

**2. What is a working envelope?**
   a) The safe operating area
   b) The robot's speed
   c) The robot's weight
   d) The robot's cost

**3. Why is guarding important?**
   a) To protect operators from hazards
   b) To make it look better
   c) To make it faster
   d) It's not important

**4. What are jigs and fixtures used for?**
   a) To present parts in consistent positions
   b) To move the robot
   c) To power the robot
   d) To program the robot

**5. What is cycle time?**
   a) Time for one complete operation
   b) The robot's age
   c) The number of joints
   d) The robot's speed

**6. What is a bottleneck?**
   a) The slowest part limiting overall throughput
   b) The fastest part
   c) The robot's base
   d) The end effector

**7. Why is operator access important?**
   a) For loading/unloading and maintenance
   b) It's not important
   c) To make it faster
   d) To change the color

**8. What is changeover time?**
   a) Time to switch between different tasks/products
   b) The robot's speed
   c) The robot's position
   d) The robot's weight

**9. How can a robot integrate with a conveyor?**
   a) Through signals and data exchange
   b) They can't integrate
   c) Only physically
   d) Only visually

**10. Why is throughput analysis important?**
   a) To optimize production efficiency
   b) It's not important
   c) To make it slower
   d) To increase cost

---

## Answer Key

1. **b)** A complete workstation with robot, fixtures, and safety systems
2. **a)** The safe operating area
3. **a)** To protect operators from hazards
4. **a)** To present parts in consistent positions
5. **a)** Time for one complete operation
6. **a)** The slowest part limiting overall throughput
7. **a)** For loading/unloading and maintenance
8. **a)** Time to switch between different tasks/products
9. **a)** Through signals and data exchange
10. **a)** To optimize production efficiency
