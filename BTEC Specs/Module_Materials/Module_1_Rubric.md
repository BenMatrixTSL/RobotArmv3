## Detailed Marking Guide

### Part 1: Understanding Robot Arms (20 marks)

#### Question 1: Basic Terminology (8 marks)
- **4 marks:** All 4 terms correct
- **3 marks:** 3 terms correct
- **2 marks:** 2 terms correct
- **1 mark:** 1 term correct
- **0 marks:** No correct answers

**Correct answers:**
- a) joint
- b) link
- c) end effector
- d) degrees of freedom

#### Question 2: Industrial Applications (12 marks)
- **4 marks per application:** Correct application with clear explanation
- **2 marks per application:** Correct application but vague explanation
- **1 mark per application:** Incorrect application or no explanation
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /20

---

### Part 2: Safety Identification (25 marks)

#### Question 3: Hazard Identification (15 marks)
- **5 marks per hazard:** Clear identification with location and who could be harmed
- **3 marks per hazard:** Hazard identified but missing details
- **1 mark per hazard:** Vague or incorrect identification
- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 4: Safe Operating Procedures (10 marks)
- **2.5 marks per answer:** Complete and correct answer
- **1.5 marks per answer:** Partially correct but incomplete
- **0.5 marks per answer:** Attempted but incorrect
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /25

---

### Part 3: Risk Assessment (30 marks)

#### Question 5: Risk Assessment Table (20 marks)
- **5 marks per hazard row:** Complete with appropriate ratings and control measures
- **3 marks per hazard row:** Mostly complete but missing some elements
- **1 mark per hazard row:** Incomplete or inappropriate ratings
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Assessment criteria:**
- Likelihood and severity ratings should be appropriate
- Risk level should reflect likelihood × severity
- Control measures should be practical and effective

#### Question 6: Control Measures (10 marks)
- **4 marks:** Three appropriate, well-explained control measures
- **3 marks:** Three control measures but some lack detail
- **2 marks:** Two control measures provided
- **1 mark:** One control measure provided
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /30

---

### Part 4: Practical Exercise (15 marks)

#### Exercise 1: Safety Walkthrough (8 marks)
- **8 marks:** All checklist items completed, detailed notes provided
- **6 marks:** Most items completed, some notes provided
- **4 marks:** Basic completion, minimal notes
- **2 marks:** Incomplete checklist
- **0 marks:** Not attempted

#### Exercise 2: Emergency Stop Practice (7 marks)
- **7 marks:** Completed three attempts, thoughtful reflection on learning
- **5 marks:** Completed attempts but limited reflection
- **3 marks:** Completed some attempts
- **1 mark:** Attempted but not completed
- **0 marks:** Not attempted

**Mark:** /15

---

### Part 5: Reflection (10 marks)

#### Question 7: Safety Reflection (10 marks)
- **4 marks per question:** Thoughtful, well-explained answers showing deep understanding
- **3 marks per question:** Good answers showing understanding
- **2 marks per question:** Basic answers, some understanding
- **1 mark per question:** Limited answers, little understanding
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /10

## Overall Performance Levels

### Excellent (85-100%)
- Demonstrates comprehensive understanding of all safety concepts
- Consistently follows all safety procedures
- Complete and accurate risk assessment
- Thoughtful reflection showing deep understanding
- All practical exercises completed to high standard

### Good (70-84%)
- Shows good understanding with minor gaps
- Follows most safety procedures correctly
- Good risk assessment with mostly appropriate measures
- Good reflection showing understanding
- Most practical exercises completed well

### Satisfactory (55-69%)
- Basic understanding evident but some confusion
- Follows basic procedures but needs reminders
- Basic risk assessment completed but lacks detail
- Basic reflection, some understanding
- Practical exercises completed but may lack detail

### Needs Improvement (Below 55%)
- Limited understanding, significant gaps
- Struggles with safety procedures
- Incomplete or inaccurate risk assessment
- Limited reflection, little understanding
- Practical exercises incomplete or not attempted
