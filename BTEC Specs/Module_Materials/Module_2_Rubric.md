## Detailed Marking Guide

### Part 1: Understanding Mechanisms (15 marks)

#### Question 1: Types of Joints (4 marks)
- **4 marks:** Both correct
- **2 marks:** One correct
- **0 marks:** Both incorrect or no answer

**Correct answers:**
- Revolute Joint: B (rotational motion)
- Prismatic Joint: A (linear motion)

#### Question 2: Degrees of Freedom (11 marks)
- **3 marks:** Correct joint count
- **3 marks:** Correct degrees of freedom
- **5 marks:** Clear explanation of relationship

**Mark:** /15

---

### Part 2: Robot Arm Structure (25 marks)

#### Question 3: Link and Joint Identification (15 marks)
- **2.5 marks per link:** Complete and accurate information
- **1.5 marks per link:** Mostly complete but missing some details
- **0.5 marks per link:** Attempted but incorrect
- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 4: Joint Movement (10 marks)
- **2.5 marks per joint:** Accurate axis and range description
- **1.5 marks per joint:** Partially correct
- **0.5 marks per joint:** Attempted but incorrect
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /25

---

### Part 3: Motion Relationships (15 marks)

#### Question 5: Joint Rotation to End Effector Motion (9 marks)
- **3 marks per answer:** Clear and correct explanation
- **2 marks per answer:** Mostly correct but lacks clarity
- **1 mark per answer:** Attempted but incorrect
- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 6: Simple Diagram (6 marks)
- **6 marks:** Complete diagram with all required elements, clear and accurate
- **4 marks:** Diagram mostly complete, minor omissions
- **2 marks:** Basic diagram but missing key elements
- **0 marks:** No diagram or completely incorrect

**Mark:** /15

---

### Part 4: Materials and Structure (20 marks)

#### Question 7: Material Identification (12 marks)
- **3 marks per component:** Correct material with appropriate justification
- **2 marks per component:** Correct material but weak justification
- **1 mark per component:** Incorrect material or no justification
- **0 marks:** No answer

#### Question 8: Material Properties (8 marks)
- **3 marks per property:** Clear and correct explanation
- **2 marks per property:** Mostly correct explanation
- **1 mark per property:** Basic understanding shown
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /20

---

### Part 5: Failure Modes (15 marks)

#### Question 9: Types of Failure (6 marks)
- **2 marks per match:** All correct
- **1 mark per match:** Some correct
- **0 marks:** All incorrect or no answer

**Correct answers:**
- Bending: C (deforming under load)
- Backlash: A (play in joints)
- Fatigue: B (repeated stress)

#### Question 10: Design Solutions (9 marks)
- **3 marks per answer:** Clear explanation of how design addresses failure mode
- **2 marks per answer:** Good explanation but lacks detail
- **1 mark per answer:** Basic understanding shown
- **0 marks:** No answer

**Mark:** /15

---

### Part 6: Practical Exercise (20 marks)

#### Exercise 1: Physical Inspection (10 marks)
- **10 marks:** All checklist items completed, detailed notes
- **7 marks:** Most items completed, some notes
- **4 marks:** Basic completion, minimal notes
- **2 marks:** Incomplete checklist
- **0 marks:** Not attempted

#### Exercise 2: Range of Motion Measurement (10 marks)
- **10 marks:** Accurate measurements for all joints, thoughtful observations
- **7 marks:** Mostly accurate measurements, some observations
- **4 marks:** Basic measurements completed
- **2 marks:** Incomplete measurements
- **0 marks:** Not attempted

**Mark:** /20

---

### Part 7: Reflection (10 marks)

#### Question 11: Understanding Mechanisms (10 marks)
- **4 marks per answer:** Thoughtful, well-explained answers showing deep understanding
- **3 marks per answer:** Good answers showing understanding
- **2 marks per answer:** Basic answers, some understanding

## Overall Performance Levels

### Excellent (85-100%)
- Comprehensive understanding of all mechanical concepts
- Accurate identification and measurement of all components
- Clear analysis of motion relationships and material choices
- Complete, accurate, and well-organized documentation
- All practical exercises completed to high standard

### Good (70-84%)
- Good understanding with minor gaps
- Mostly accurate identification and measurement
- Good analysis with minor gaps
- Good documentation with minor omissions
- Most practical exercises completed well

### Satisfactory (55-69%)
- Basic understanding evident but some confusion
- Basic identification with some errors
- Basic analysis completed
- Basic documentation completed
- Practical exercises completed but may lack detail

### Needs Improvement (Below 55%)
- Limited understanding, significant gaps
- Struggles with identification and measurement
- Limited or incorrect analysis
- Incomplete or disorganized documentation
- Practical exercises incomplete or not attempted
